Study Skills: Lectures & Tutorials

Listening To Lectures

One of the main ways in which information is communicated at university is through lectures. Lectures highlight and reinforce essential knowledge in particular disciplines, guide your reading and research and stimulate your interest in the subject.

Preparing for Lecture

  • Making yourself familiar with the topic before attending the lecture.
  • Having a copy of readings before you attend the lecture.
  • Be prepared with all the stuffs you may need.

During the lecture

  • Try to sit in the first few rows of the lecture room as you will find it easier to focus on the lecture.
  • Listen carefully to the outline of the lecture to have the idea about topics.
  • Become involved to the teacher and be active to brainstorm.

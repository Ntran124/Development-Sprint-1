Lectures

Lectures are a key feature of university teaching. In broad terms, the principal purpose of a lecture is to provide you with information in an efficient, well-structured way.

Preparation

Effective tutorial or seminar presentations are the outcome of careful planning and thoughtful preparation. You will need to gather information by consulting the appropriate literature.

Tutorials

Tutorials are meetings of smaller groups of students to discuss specific topics related to the subject matter of the course. Before the tutorials, read articles from your subject guide.

Laboratory Practicals

Practical provides the opportunity for students to observe and manipulate materials. Before the practical, complete any required reading and ensure you have any necessary equipment.

Content and organisation

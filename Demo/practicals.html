Practicals

Practicals provide the opportunity for students to observe and manipulate materials and give insight into scientific methods and processes as well as scientific knowledge. Before the practical, complete any required reading and ensure you have any necessary equipment necessary. During the practical, ensure that you listen to and follow the lecturerâ€™s instructions. Your notes should cover:

Notes to consider

  • Aim of the practical.
  • Safety Instructions.
  • Details about the equipments & materials.
  • Details about the method.
  • Results of the exercise/experiment.
  • Important incidental suggestions made by the demonstrator.
